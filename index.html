    Freecycling! Activities

    Participate in local 'Freecycling!' flea markets

    Every month, we hold local flea markets! You can freely bring your items that you no longer use and share those with anyone visiting the flea markets!

    Find someone who needs your items
    in 'Freecycling' network

    You can post any items online at any time! If someone wants to get your items, you can arrange time and location with them and trade one to one.

    Donate your items to the local 'Freecycling!' shop

    If you don't have time to participate in flea markets or trade one to one, you can donate your clean and intact items to our local shops.
